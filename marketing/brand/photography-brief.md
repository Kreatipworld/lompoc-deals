# Lompoc Deals — Photography Brief
*Version: 1.0 | Date: 2026-04-08 | Owner: CMO*
*Status: Ready for photographer/creative team*

---

## Purpose

Replace emoji and gradient placeholder imagery in the website mockups with real, warm-light photography that communicates Lompoc community identity. Photos will be used across the homepage, deal cards, merchant spotlights, and social media.

---

## Brand Constraints (Non-Negotiable)

- **Warm natural light only** — golden hour preferred. No harsh midday sun, no cold/clinical lighting.
- **Real Lompoc locations** — recognizable streets (H Street, Ocean Ave), La Purisima, Jalama Beach, Lompoc murals, Flower Festival imagery if available/licensed.
- **People-forward** — real community members (diverse, bilingual community). No obvious stock photo poses.
- **No staged perfection** — authentic warmth. A slightly imperfect moment beats a plastic "diverse team" stock shot every time.
- **Spanish speakers included naturally** — not tokenized. If a family is eating together, that's the shot.

---

## Color Direction

Photos should feel warm and earthy — they will sit on Cream White (#FAF7F2) backgrounds. Avoid:
- Cold/blue-tinted lighting
- Pure white backgrounds (feels clinical)
- High-contrast dark backgrounds (reserve for hero overlays only)

---

## Shot List by Use Case

### 1. Hero Background (Homepage + /for-merchants)
- **Concept**: Wide, atmospheric Lompoc scene that communicates "this is your town"
- **Options**:
  - La Purisima Mission at golden hour (wide, warm, timeless)
  - H Street merchants setting up in morning light
  - Lompoc valley farmland at dusk with Flower Festival color reference
- **Specs**: 1440×900px minimum, horizontal, people optional (bokeh bg OK)
- **Usage**: Dark gradient overlay applied in CSS — doesn't need to be perfectly exposed

### 2. Merchant Spotlight Photos (Community Spotlight section)
- **Concept**: Portrait-style photo of a local merchant in their business
- **Shot**: Merchant behind counter, in their kitchen, at their salon chair, etc.
- **Feeling**: Proud, relaxed, at home in their business
- **Examples by category**:
  - Restaurant: chef/owner plating food, warm kitchen light
  - Salon: stylist smiling with client in chair
  - Retail: owner behind counter, product in foreground
  - Dispensary: professional staff, clean environment
  - Winery: winemaker in vineyard or tasting room
- **Specs**: 800×600px minimum, face clearly visible, horizontal
- **Count needed**: 6–8 merchant portraits for initial library

### 3. Deal/Product Lifestyle Photos (Deal Cards)
- **Concept**: The experience of the deal, not just the product
- **Examples**:
  - Food deal: plate of food in warm light, fork in action — NOT a studio food shot
  - Beauty deal: satisfied client after treatment, natural smile
  - Automotive: car being detailed, technician at work
  - Home service: contractor shaking homeowner's hand at doorstep
- **Specs**: 400×280px minimum (deal card image area), horizontal
- **Count needed**: 10–15 lifestyle shots covering main deal categories

### 4. Community / Local Color Photos (Social Media + Blog)
- **Concept**: What makes Lompoc, Lompoc
- **Shots needed**:
  - Lompoc Flower Festival (sweet peas, parade, community gathering)
  - H Street storefronts — charming, local, not generic
  - Vandenberg Space Force Base skyline (community context)
  - Local family eating at a restaurant (candid, warm)
  - Nextdoor neighbors chatting (community feel)
  - La Purisima Mission or Jalama Beach (natural beauty + local pride)
- **Count needed**: 8–12 atmospheric shots

## Licensing Requirements

- **All images must be licensed for commercial use** — website, social, and paid ads
- Model releases required for any identifiable person
- Location releases required for private businesses
- Preferred: full buyout for primary shots; editorial license acceptable for atmospheric shots
