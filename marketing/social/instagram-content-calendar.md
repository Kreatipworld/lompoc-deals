**Frequency:** 3x/week  
**Format mix per week:**
- 1 Deal Highlight (Reel or static card)
- 1 Merchant Story (Reel or carousel — introduce a local business)
- 1 Community Post (poll, question, local tip, or user-generated content)

**Languages:** Alternate EN/ES posts. Every caption gets both languages (EN first, ES below with 🇲🇽 separator).

## Engagement Tactics

1. **Reply to every comment** within 24 hours — especially in first 2 weeks (IG algorithm rewards this)
2. **Follow local accounts:** Lompoc businesses, Lompoc Record, local influencers, Lompoc Chamber
3. **Story daily (even simple):** Poll, countdown, deal-of-the-day sticker → drives DMs → followers
4. **Tag merchants** in every deal highlight post — they often reshare (free amplification)
5. **Instagram Reels get 2–3x organic reach** vs static posts — prioritize Reels for deal highlights
