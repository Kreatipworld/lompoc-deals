*Owner: CMO / Social & Community Manager*

---

## Market Context

- **Santa Rita Hills AVA** — world-class Pinot Noir and Chardonnay region; established wine tourism draw
- **Lompoc Wine Ghetto** — industrial-chic urban tasting room district; unique Instagram/TikTok angle
- **20 wineries now listed** on Lompoc Deals with dedicated homepage tab
- Wine tourism audience: higher income, shares content readily, converts to deals (tasting discounts, pairing events)
- Seasonal peaks: **harvest (Aug–Oct)**, **Valentine's Day**, **Mother's Day**, **Memorial Day weekend**

---

## Content Pillars (3 repeating themes)

1. **"Deal of the Week" — Winery Edition:** Feature a tasting deal or special from a listed winery. Drives direct traffic to deal page.
2. **"Lompoc Wine Country Guide":** Educational/tourism content about Santa Rita Hills, Wine Ghetto, visiting tips. Shareable, SEO-friendly.
3. **Winery Spotlight:** Introduce a specific winery — their story, what makes them unique, their current deal. Builds merchant loyalty.

---

## Platform Notes

- **Instagram Reels** — best for winery visuals (vineyard rows, tasting room interiors, glasses of wine). 15–30 sec.
- **TikTok** — "hidden gem" and "you didn't know about this" hooks perform well for wine tourism
- **Pinterest** — high intent for wine tourism searches; pin every post with SEO-optimized descriptions
- **Facebook** — share to Lompoc wine and Santa Barbara wine enthusiast groups
- **Hashtags:** #LompocWine #SantaRitaHills #LompocWineCountry #WineGhetto #LompocDeals #SantaBarbaraWine #CaliforniaWine #WineTourism
