## Platform Notes

- Nextdoor skews 35–65+, homeowners, higher local trust than Facebook
- Posts appear in neighborhood feeds — write as a neighbor, not a brand
- Avoid heavy promotional language ("SALE!", "$$$") — gets flagged or ignored
- Always include the URL as plain text (Nextdoor strips most links)
- Spanish version: post separately in same thread as a reply, or as a separate post if you have a Spanish-speaking neighbor identity
- Best posting times: Tue/Thu 7–9 AM and 6–8 PM Pacific
- Post frequency: 1–2x/week max (Nextdoor penalizes over-posting from business accounts)
