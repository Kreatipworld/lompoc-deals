## Why This Matters

Vandenberg rocket launches — especially night Falcon 9 launches — go massively viral on TikTok and Instagram. The "jellyfish" plasma cloud effect from Falcon 9's upper-stage engine burn creates a stunning visual that gets millions of views. People from across California tag "#Vandenberg" and "#rocketlaunch" looking for local context.

**The opportunity:** Post within minutes of a launch and ride the hashtag wave. A 15-second video from anywhere in Lompoc — showing the streak in the sky or just the glow on the horizon — with local context = 10k+ views, easy.

**The merchant tie-in:** "Watching the launch from Lompoc? Here's where to eat/drink beforehand" → drives traffic to local businesses on the platform.

## Launch Tracking

**How to know when a launch is coming:**
- Follow [@SpaceX](https://twitter.com/SpaceX) on X/Twitter — they announce T-24h and T-1h
- Follow [@30thSpaceWing](https://twitter.com/30thSpaceWing) and [@VandenbergSFB](https://twitter.com/VandenbergSFB)
- Subscribe to launch alerts at spaceflightnow.com/launch-schedule

**Best viewing from Lompoc:**
- Ocean Avenue (west side of town, unobstructed western horizon)
- Harris Grade Road (elevated, city lights behind you)
- Any open field on the west side of town

**Night launches** (after 9pm) are most photogenic — the plasma cloud is bright enough to photograph with a phone.
