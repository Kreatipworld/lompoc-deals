## Compliance Notes (IMPORTANT — Read Before Outreach)

California cannabis advertising regulations (Business & Professions Code §26152):
- ✅ Allowed: Promote deals, prices, product types, discounts
- ❌ Not allowed: Health benefit claims ("relieves pain," "cures anxiety")
- ❌ Not allowed: Targeting people under 21
- ⚠️ Required: Age gate or disclaimer if cannabis content is publicly visible

**Before running any paid ads mentioning cannabis businesses:** confirm with the board that the site has adequate age-gating in the cannabis category. For organic outreach and listings, standard compliance applies.
