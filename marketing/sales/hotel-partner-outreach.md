## Pitch Talking Points (All Hotels)

1. **"Your property is already listed — with a map."** Zero resistance opener. Show them the URL.
2. **"472 Lompoc businesses are already on our interactive map."** Scale signals legitimacy.
3. **"We just published a hotels guide that ranks for 'hotels in Lompoc CA' searches."** Direct traffic proof.
4. **"Deal alerts go directly to subscribers — no algorithm."** Email delivery is a real hook for hotel marketers used to OTA dependency.
5. **"No booking commission. Flat $19.99/month."** Immediately differentiates from Expedia/Booking.com.
6. **"Start free — upgrade whenever."** Zero barrier to entry.
