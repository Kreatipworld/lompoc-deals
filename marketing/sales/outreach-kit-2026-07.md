## The 10-second pitch

> **EN:** "Your business is already on Lompoc Locals — photos, hours, everything. Locals are already viewing your page. Claiming it is free and takes two minutes — want the link?"
>
> **ES:** "Su negocio ya está en Lompoc Locals — fotos, horario, todo. Los vecinos ya están viendo su página. Reclamarlo es gratis y toma dos minutos — ¿le mando el enlace?"

**Why this works:** you're not selling — you're *returning something that's theirs*. Every prospect's profile URL is in the CSV; open it on your phone and show them. That's the whole demo.

The paid follow-up (once Stripe is live):

> "Want your offer on the Deal Board and in the Saturday email? That's the Growth plan — $39.99 a month, first deal takes two minutes to post, and you see exactly how many people claimed it."
