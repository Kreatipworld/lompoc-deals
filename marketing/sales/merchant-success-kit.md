# Your First 30 Days on Lompoc Deals

Welcome to Lompoc Deals — Lompoc's free local business directory. This guide covers exactly what to do in your first month to get real visibility, real foot traffic, and real results.

It takes about 2 hours total. Most of it you do once and never touch again.

---

## Week 1 — Get Found (Days 1–7)

### ✅ Step 1: Complete Your Profile (30 min)

A complete profile gets 3× more views than an incomplete one. Do these before anything else:

**Must-haves (your listing won't rank well without these):**
- [ ] Business name, address, phone number — exact as on Google
- [ ] Cover photo (1200×630px minimum — your storefront, product, or team)
- [ ] Logo or profile photo
- [ ] Business description (2–3 sentences: what you do, who you serve, what makes you different)
- [ ] Hours of operation
- [ ] Category (choose the most accurate one)

**High-value additions:**
- [ ] Website URL
- [ ] Instagram or Facebook link
- [ ] Spanish description (63% of Lompoc speaks Spanish — bilingual listings get more engagement)

**Quick description template** (fill in the blanks):
> "[Business Name] is Lompoc's [what you do] serving [who you serve] since [year]. We [what makes you different — price, quality, experience, specialty]. Visit us at [address] or call [phone]."

**Spanish template:**
> "[Nombre del negocio] es el/la [qué hacen] de Lompoc, atendiendo a [a quién sirven] desde [año]. Ofrecemos [qué los hace especiales]. Visítenos en [dirección] o llámenos al [teléfono]."

---

### ✅ Step 2: Post Your First Deal (15 min)

Deals are what drive consumer clicks. You don't need a big discount — even a modest offer gets people to click and remember you.

**Deal ideas by business type:**

| Type | Easy first deal |
|------|----------------|
| Restaurant / Café | "Free dessert with any entrée — mention Lompoc Deals" |
| Salon / Beauty | "10% off first visit for new clients" |
| Retail | "Buy 2, get 1 free on [product category]" |
| Auto / Service | "Free inspection with any service over $50" |
| Winery / Bar | "Complimentary second tasting for groups of 4+" |
| Dispensary | "15% off your first order — show this deal" |
| Fitness / Wellness | "First class free" or "Free 30-min consult" |

**Deal writing tips:**
- Keep it simple — one clear offer, one clear action
- Set an expiration date (creates urgency, even if you renew it)
- Include "mention Lompoc Deals" or "show this deal" so you know it's working
- EN + ES if possible (reach both audiences)

---

### ✅ Step 3: Tell Your Existing Customers (10 min)

Your regulars are your best early reviewers and referrers. Let them know you're on the directory:

**Instagram Story / Facebook post:**
> "We're now on Lompoc Deals! Browse our profile and grab our current deal — link in bio." (tag @lompocdeals if the account exists)

**Text to loyal customers (if you have their numbers):**
> "Hey, we just joined Lompoc Deals — Lompoc's local business directory. Check out our profile and grab our current deal: [your direct URL]. Thanks for your support!"

**In-store sign or table card:**
> "Find us on Lompoc Deals → lompoc-deals.vercel.app/biz/[your-slug]"
> "Encuéntranos en Lompoc Deals → lompoc-deals.vercel.app/biz/[your-slug]"

## Week 2 — Build Momentum (Days 8–14)

### ✅ Step 4: Update Your Deal or Add a Second One

A fresh deal signals to the directory (and to customers browsing) that you're active. Update weekly if possible, or at minimum every 2 weeks.

**Rotation ideas:**
- Week 1: New-customer discount
- Week 2: Bundle deal ("Combo especial")
- Week 3: Loyalty offer ("Reward for regulars")
- Week 4: Limited-time special (tied to a holiday, event, or season)

On the **Free tier**, you can post up to 3 active deals. Use all 3 if you can — more deals = more reasons for people to click your profile.

---

### ✅ Step 5: Add Your Business to Google (if not already done)

Lompoc Deals lists you locally, but Google Business Profile (GBP) reaches people searching the whole web. They work together — not in competition.

**5-minute GBP check:**
1. Search your business name on Google
2. If a Knowledge Panel appears on the right: great, you're claimed
3. If not: go to business.google.com and claim your listing
4. Make sure your name, address, phone, and hours on GBP **exactly match** Lompoc Deals

Consistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone) across both platforms improves your local SEO ranking.

---

### ✅ Step 6: Ask for Your First Review (5 min)

Businesses with reviews get more clicks. You don't need many — even 3–5 genuine reviews make a difference.

**Script for asking in person:**
> "Thanks for coming in! We just joined Lompoc Deals — if you have 2 minutes, a quick review on our profile would really help us out. You can find it at lompoc-deals.vercel.app/biz/[your-slug]."

**Text/WhatsApp message:**
> "Hi [Name], thanks for your recent visit! We'd love a quick review on our Lompoc Deals profile if you have a moment: [your URL]. Means a lot — gracias!"

---

## Week 3–4 — Go Deeper (Days 15–30)

### ✅ Step 7: Check Your Views and Clicks

On your merchant dashboard, you can see how many people have viewed your profile and clicked on your deals. Check it once a week.

**What to look for:**
- **Views trending up?** Your profile is being found. Keep doing what you're doing.
- **Lots of views, few deal clicks?** Your deal might need a stronger offer or clearer copy. Try adjusting it.
- **Few views overall?** Try refreshing your cover photo or updating your description.

## Common Questions

**"Do I need to be a premium member to show up in the directory?"**
No. Every business gets a free listing in the directory. Deals are optional — even a profile-only listing gets you found by locals searching your category.

**"How do customers find my deal?"**
Your deal shows up on the homepage feed, in your category page, and on your business profile. Customers can browse without an account.

**"Can I pause my deals without deleting them?"**
Yes — in your merchant dashboard you can toggle deals active/inactive at any time. Great for seasonal businesses or when you're between promotions.

**"What if I want to change my deal offer?"**
Edit it directly from the dashboard. Changes take effect immediately.

**"I'm not getting any views — what should I do?"**
Start with your cover photo — it's the #1 driver of clicks on category browse pages. A clear, well-lit photo of your product, service, or storefront makes a dramatic difference. Second: make sure your category is set correctly so you appear in the right category browse page.
