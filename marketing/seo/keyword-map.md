## Target URLs — Current Site Structure

*Note: Site uses `/category/[slug]` (not `/lompoc/[slug]`). REQ-005 would add programmatic `/lompoc/[category]` routes for additional SEO surface.*

**Live now:**
- `/` — homepage directory (primary landing for discovery queries)
- `/deals` — all active deals (primary landing for deals/coupon queries)
- `/category/food-drink` — restaurants and dining
- `/category/health-beauty` — salons, spas, wellness
- `/category/services` — professional services
- `/category/retail` — shops and retail
- `/category/automotive` — auto repair and dealerships
- `/category/wineries` — wine tasting and wineries
- `/category/dispensaries` — cannabis dispensaries
- `/category/real-estate` — real estate
- `/for-businesses` — merchant acquisition landing page
- `/biz/[slug]` — individual business profile pages ← high long-tail SEO value

**Planned (REQ-005):**
- `/lompoc` — hub page with all categories + counts
- `/lompoc/[category-slug]` — city + category programmatic pages

## On-Page SEO Title Templates (Updated)

| Page type | Title pattern |
|-----------|--------------|
| Homepage | `Lompoc Local Directory — Find Businesses, Deals & Things To Do in Lompoc, CA` |
| Category page | `[Category] in Lompoc, CA — Local Businesses & Deals \| Lompoc Deals` |
| Business profile | `[Business Name] — Deals & Coupons in Lompoc, CA \| Lompoc Deals` |
| /deals page | `Lompoc Deals & Coupons — Browse Local Specials \| Lompoc Deals` |
| /for-businesses | `Get Your Lompoc Business Listed Free — Be Found by Locals \| Lompoc Deals` |
| Search results | `Search Results for "[query]" in Lompoc, CA \| Lompoc Deals` |

**On-Page Checklist (per page):**
- [ ] `<title>`: follows template above (directory-first, not deals-first)
- [ ] `<meta description>`: 150–160 chars, include city + category + free to browse
- [ ] H1 includes primary keyword
- [ ] LocalBusiness schema markup (name, address, URL, openingHours, priceRange)
- [ ] Breadcrumb schema on category + business pages
- [ ] Internal links to: homepage, category siblings, related business profiles
- [ ] Canonical URL set
- [ ] Open Graph tags + lompoc-hero.jpg OG image
- [ ] Dispensary pages: 21+ age gate meta note + compliance badge
