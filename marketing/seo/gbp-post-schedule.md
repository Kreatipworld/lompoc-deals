*CMO | 2026-04-11 | Pairs with: marketing/seo/gbp-setup-checklist.md*

---

## Overview

GBP posts show in Google Search and Maps when someone searches "Lompoc Deals" or "local businesses Lompoc." Posts expire after 7 days — post once a week, every Tuesday, to maintain fresh content.

**Post weekly on:** Tuesday 9am PT  
**Post type:** "What's New" (most versatile)  
**Max length:** 1,500 characters (keep to ~300 for scan-readability)  
**CTA button:** "Learn more" → lompoc-deals.vercel.app

## GBP Post Performance Notes

- GBP posts with photos get 2–3× more views than text-only — always add an image
- "What's New" posts outperform "Offer" posts for awareness (save Offer type for specific time-limited deals)
- Track clicks via Google Business Profile Insights (Performance → Calls/Website clicks)
- Once 50+ merchants are listed, switch to featuring a specific merchant deal weekly — most engaging format for return visits
