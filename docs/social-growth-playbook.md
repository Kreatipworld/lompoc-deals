# Lompoc Locals — Social Growth Playbook (Organic)

Goal: grow a Lompoc consumer audience on TikTok + Instagram, then convert that audience into paying business clients ($39.99 Growth / $99.99 Plus, digest ads $49/wk).

Method: Post Bridge organic growth system (warmup → content-market fit → trends → conversion → scale), adapted for a two-sided local platform.

**Core insight:** Lompoc Locals is two-sided. Businesses pay for exposure to locals — so the consumer audience isn't marketing, it IS the product being sold. Every audience milestone becomes sales collateral.

**Local math:** Lompoc is ~44K people. A 3–5K view video is massive market penetration. Comments come from actual future customers, not strangers.

---

## Phase 1 — Account creation (Day 1)

- Create a NEW Instagram **business** account: `@lompoclocals` (business type unlocks the bio link).
- Create a NEW TikTok account: `@lompoclocals`.
- Do not reuse old accounts with unrelated history.

**Bio (both platforms):**

> Deals, garage sales & local events in Lompoc, CA 💜💛
> By locals, for locals. Para la comunidad.
> 👇 This week's deals

Link: `https://www.lompoclocals.com`. TikTok won't allow a clickable link at first — type the domain as text.

Do NOT put pricing or "businesses: sign up" in the bio yet. Audience first; the business line gets added after Day 30 (see Phase 5).

## Phase 2 — Warmup (Days 2–8) — DO NOT POST

Skipping warmup is the #1 cause of zero-view accounts. Seven days, engagement only.

Daily protocol, 15 min per platform:

1. Scroll ONLY local-niche content: Lompoc, Santa Barbara County, Central Coast, local food reviews, garage-sale hauls, "things to do in [small town]" videos.
2. Follow local accounts: businesses, community pages, school pages, local creators.
3. Like, watch fully, leave genuine comments.
4. From Day 4 on: save 3–5 viral videos per day (100K+ views) whose *format* could be remade for Lompoc.

**Exit goal: 15–25 saved viral videos as content templates.**

## Phase 3 — Content-market fit (Days 8–30)

One video per day. Remake saved viral formats with Lompoc content — never invent from scratch.

Rules:
- First 10 posts per account: posted manually from the **mobile app**.
- Every video gets a natural CTA (caption or pinned comment): "full list on lompoclocals.com".
- Reply to every comment — each unanswered comment is lost reach and a lost lead.
- Judge by only two metrics: **watch time** and **comments**.
- No videos that look like ads. Educate, entertain, connect.
- Commit to the volume: winning formats often take 50–100+ attempts.

Starter formats (adapt hooks from your saved templates):

| Format | Cadence | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| "Garage sales in Lompoc this Saturday" | Fri/Sat | Screen-record the lompoclocals.com map, voiceover top 3 |
| "$20 in Lompoc" challenge | Weekly | Stack real deals from the feed |
| Hidden gem business spotlight | 2×/week | 30s on a local business, tag them — this is the client pipeline |
| "This weekend in Lompoc" | Fri | Pull from events pages |
| Local POV / small-town humor | Weekly | Builds the follower base |

When a format consistently clears 1K+ views: make variations. Kill underperformers.

## Phase 4 — Riding trends (ongoing)

- 1–2 trend adaptations per week, alongside (not replacing) proven formats.
- Look for sounds/formats with 10K–50K uses (growing, not saturated).
- Adapt to the Lompoc niche within 24–48 hours of spotting.

## Phase 5 — Views → paying business clients

Funnel: video → profile → bio → lompoclocals.com → `/for-businesses`.

1. **Feature businesses free, then pitch with receipts.** When a spotlight performs, DM or visit the owner: *"That video of your shop got 4,200 views — 1 in 10 people in Lompoc. That's what exposure on Lompoc Locals looks like. Plans start at $39.99/mo."*
2. Tagged businesses reshare to their stories → their followers discover the account → free distribution loop.
3. After Day 30 (with real numbers), add a second bio line: *"Businesses: get featured 👇"* pointing at `/for-businesses`.
4. Reuse every social win in the weekly digest ad pitch ($49/wk, $79–99/mo spots) — same inventory story.

## Phase 6 — Scaling (Day 30+, gated)

Do NOT scale until ALL are true:
- [ ] 30+ days of consistent posting
- [ ] At least one video 10K+ views (or several 3–5K locally)
- [ ] 2–3 proven repeatable formats
- [ ] At least one client conversation started from a video

Then: 2–3 posts/day on main account (3–4h apart) → second account after full 7-day warmup → cross-post winners to YouTube Shorts / Facebook (strong for local audiences) / X.

## Non-negotiable rules

1. Always 7-day warmup before posting on any new account.
2. First 10 posts per account from the mobile app, manually.
3. No automation/scheduling tools until the account averages 500+ views/video.
4. Never repost an identical video on the same platform.
5. Two metrics only: watch time and comments.
