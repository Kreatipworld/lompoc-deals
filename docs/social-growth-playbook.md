# Lompoc Locals — Social Growth Playbook

Organic TikTok + Instagram → local audience → paying business clients. No ads.

**The play:** Lompoc Locals is two-sided. Businesses pay for exposure to locals, so the follower base IS the product. Grow the audience, then pitch businesses with receipts.

**The math:** Lompoc ≈ 44K people. A 3–5K view video = 1 in 10 residents. There are already **426 businesses** in the Lompoc Locals database — that's the pitch list.

---

## Day 1 — Set up

- New Instagram **business** account + new TikTok: `@lompoclocals`
- Display name: **Lompoc Locals | Deals & Events** (name field is searchable)
- Link: `www.lompoclocals.com` (TikTok: type domain as plain text)
- Bio (both):
  > Deals, garage sales & local events in Lompoc, CA 💜💛
  > By locals, for locals. Para la comunidad.
  > 👇 This week's deals
- No pricing / "businesses sign up" in bio until Day 30.

## Days 2–8 — Warmup (NO POSTING, 15 min/day per platform)

Posting on a cold account = buried at 0–100 views. Non-negotiable.

**Follow + engage daily:**
- @explore_lompoc (~9.6K), @cityoflompoc (~4.2K), @lompocvalleychamber (~3.5K)
- Local restaurants, wineries, thrift shops, school pages, local creators
- Search and scroll: #lompoc #lompocca #lompocvalley #explorelompoc #vandenberg #centralcoast #805

**From Day 4:** save 3–5 viral videos/day (100K+ views) whose format works for Lompoc. Exit goal: **15–25 saved templates**.

## Days 8–30 — One video per day

- **Remake, don't invent** — copy the hook + structure of a saved template, swap in Lompoc.
- Film on phone, edit in CapCut, post from the mobile app (first 10 posts minimum).
- CTA on every video (caption or pinned comment): "full list on lompoclocals.com".
- Reply to **every** comment.
- Judge by watch time + comments only. Format clears 1K+ views twice → make variations.
- 1–2 trend adaptations/week (sounds at 10K–50K uses, within 48h).
- Expect 50–100+ attempts before a reliable winner. No quitting before Day 30.

## Views → clients (runs from Week 2)

1. Feature 2 businesses/week free. Tag them, make them look great.
2. Spotlight performs → screenshot views + comments → pitch within 48h:
   > "That video of your shop got 4,200 views — 1 in 10 people in Lompoc. That's what exposure on Lompoc Locals looks like. Plans start at $39.99/mo. lompoclocals.com/for-businesses"
3. Ask tagged businesses to reshare to their story.
4. Track: business, views, pitched Y/N, response.
5. Day 30: add bio line "Businesses: get featured 👇"; reuse view counts in digest ad pitches ($49/wk).

## Scaling gate (Day 30+ — ALL required)

- [ ] 30+ days consistent posting
- [ ] One 10K+ video (or several 3–5K)
- [ ] 2–3 repeatable formats
- [ ] 1+ client conversation from a video

Then: 2–3 posts/day → second account (own 7-day warmup) → cross-post to YouTube Shorts + Facebook.

## Non-negotiables

1. 7-day warmup on any new account. 2. First 10 posts from the phone app. 3. No schedulers under 500 avg views. 4. Never repost identical videos. 5. Watch time + comments are the only metrics.
