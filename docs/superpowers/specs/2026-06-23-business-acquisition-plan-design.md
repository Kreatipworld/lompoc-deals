# Lompoc Deals — Business Acquisition Plan (Concierge Beachhead)

**Date:** 2026-06-23
**Owner:** Andres (solo, ~3 hrs/week)
**Phase:** 1 of 2 — recruit the supply side (businesses) before pushing the demand side (locals)

---

## Strategic frame

At a few hours a week, **conversations are the bottleneck, not content.** The plan
optimizes for getting a *small number of businesses truly live and happy*, then mining
each one for social proof and reach. We ignore the ~270 scraped directory listings as a
mass-outreach target for now — they're seeded data, not relationships.

**Sequencing:** Businesses first (supply), then locals (demand). "Claim your free listing"
is an easier ask than "change your shopping habits," and once businesses are live we can
tell locals there are real deals, then tell the *next* businesses that locals are already
subscribed.

**Motion chosen:** Concierge beachhead (do-it-for-them onboarding of a hand-picked 25),
with a thin templated email layer (option B) deferred to Phase 2, and a light always-on
content cadence so the platform looks alive when prospects check it.

---

## 1. The Offer

**"Founding Business" — free listing, done for you.**

- Free to claim and post unlimited deals.
- Scarcity framing: *"I'm setting up the first 25 Lompoc businesses myself, for free."*
- Lock-in: founding businesses stay free (or get an extended free run) if paid tiers
  launch later. Costs nothing now; makes "yes" easy.
- The real unlock is **done-for-you setup.** Owners are busy and skeptical of "another
  website." Removing all their effort beats any feature pitch.

> **Assumption to confirm:** listing is free today. If a paid tier exists, hold it until
> founding businesses are live and seeing value.

## 2. The Beachhead 25 (target list)

Build the shortlist from the scraped Google Places data, filtered by:

- **Categories that profit from repeat foot traffic:** restaurants, cafés, taco shops,
  salons/barbers, fitness/gyms, breweries & tasting rooms (incl. the Lompoc Wine Ghetto),
  auto services, boutiques.
- **Warm first:** spots Andres already frequents / owners he knows → first 5–8,
  near-guaranteed yeses.
- **Geography:** Old Town Lompoc / H Street commercial corridor is the dense, walkable
  beachhead. ZIPs 93436/37/38 only. Vandenberg-adjacent / military-family-serving
  businesses are a bonus angle.
- **Skip:** chains/franchises (no local decision-maker), anything outside the ZIPs.

**Tiering:** Tier 1 warm (≈8) · Tier 2 great-fit cold-but-local (≈12) · Tier 3 backup (≈5).

## 3. Outreach rhythm (solo, ~3 hrs/week)

| Item | Target |
|---|---|
| Weekly conversations | 5 real conversations → ~3 go live |
| Cadence | One 2-hr "walk Old Town / H Street" block + 1 hr follow-ups |
| 8-week goal | 12–15 businesses live with real deals |

**The close:** never "check out my site." Always *"Can I set you up right now? Takes 10
minutes, it's free, and I'll write your first deal for you."* Do it on the spot, on your
phone, when they say yes — momentum dies in "I'll look at it later."

## 4. Content plan (light, proof-driven — 1–2 posts/week, batched monthly)

Four pillars, all fed by onboarded businesses (no inventing content):

1. **Deal of the week** — spotlight one founding business's offer.
2. **Business spotlight** — short feature of a new founding business; **tag them so they
   reshare to their followers.** This is the free-reach engine.
3. **Lompoc local** — community flavor ("best breakfast burrito in Lompoc?") to read as a
   local, not an ad.
4. **Milestones** — "10 Lompoc businesses now on Lompoc Deals" = social proof for the next
   pitch.

**Channels:** Facebook + Instagram, leaning Facebook. Lompoc's community lives in **local
FB groups** (e.g., "Lompoc Community Board"); posting deals there where the rules allow is
the highest-leverage free channel.

## 5. Tracking & Phase 2

**Tracking sheet columns:** business · tier · status (contacted / interested / live) ·
deal posted? · did they reshare?

**Phase 1 success:** 15 live businesses with active deals + ~100 local subscribers,
~8 weeks.

**Phase 2 trigger:** once ~15 are live → flip to locals (FB groups + weekly digest push)
**and** send the thin templated email blast to second-tier businesses using the messaging
proven in person.

## Copy-paste templates

### A. In-person 30-second pitch
> "Hey — I'm building Lompoc Deals, a site just for Lompoc businesses and locals. It's a
> free spot for you to post specials and coupons, and it goes out to locals in a weekly
> email. I'm setting up the first 25 businesses myself, free, and I'd love to add you.
> Takes me 10 minutes and I'll even write your first deal — can I set you up right now?"

### B. Warm-call / DM script
> "Hi [Name], it's Andres. Quick one — I launched Lompoc Deals, a local deals site just
> for Lompoc. I'm hand-setting-up the first 25 businesses for free and thought of [Business]
> right away. Zero work on your end, I do the whole setup. Can I swing by this week, or set
> it up over the phone now?"

### C. Follow-up text (no response / "let me think")
> "No pressure [Name]! Whenever you've got 10 min I'll get [Business] live on Lompoc Deals
> and write your first deal for you — still free for founding businesses. Want me to just
> go ahead and draft it so you can approve it?"

### D. "You're live" handoff message
> "[Business] is live 🎉 Here's your page: [link]. Your first deal is [deal]. Two things
> that really help: (1) reshare the spotlight I'm posting so your followers see it, and
> (2) text me any new special anytime and I'll post it. Welcome to Lompoc Deals!"

### E. Content-pillar post formats
- **Deal of the week:** photo + "🔥 This week in Lompoc: [deal] at [business]. Tap the
  link in bio. #LompocDeals #Lompoc"
- **Business spotlight:** "Meet [business] 👋 [one line of story]. They just joined Lompoc
  Deals — go say hi at [Old Town / location]. @[their handle]"
- **Lompoc local:** question/poll post — "Best [taco / coffee / breakfast burrito] in
  Lompoc? 👇 Settle it." (engagement bait, builds local following)
- **Milestone:** "[N] Lompoc businesses are now on Lompoc Deals — and growing. If you run a
  local spot, DM us, it's free to join."

## Out of scope (Phase 1, by design — YAGNI)

- Mass email blast to all ~270 scraped businesses (deferred to Phase 2, post-proof).
- Paid ads / flyers (no budget this phase).
- Locals-side acquisition campaign (Phase 2).
- Daily content production (unsustainable solo; weekly cadence only).
