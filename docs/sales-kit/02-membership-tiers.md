## The tiers in plain terms

### Free — $0/mo
Claim your listing, drop your pin on the map, add your hours and photos, and show up when locals search your category. This is table stakes, and it costs nothing. With 476 approved businesses already on the platform, being unclaimed means being invisible next to neighbors who aren't.

### Growth — $39.99/mo
Everything in Free, plus the tools to actually pull customers in: post up to 15 deals, land in the **weekly digest** that goes out to subscribers, see your **views and clicks**, and connect your social links and Google reviews. This is the tier for a business that wants its promotions working, not just its address listed.

### Plus — "Official Partner" — $99.99/mo
Everything in Growth, plus the reach and the reputation. You get **unlimited deals**, the **Official Partner badge**, and **priority ranking — first in your category**. Your business rides the **homepage Featured Members marquee**, takes the **top-of-category slide** and the **search sponsor row**, and your coupon appears **site-wide** at the bottom of profiles, blogs, events, and garage-sale pages. You get a **gold-star map marker**, a **done-for-you curated profile** (we build it with real photos and verified details — you just approve), and **priority support**. The top Partner in each category even becomes that category's **cover photo on the homepage.**

This is how you go from "listed" to "the name people recognize."

### Category Exclusive — $249/mo ($2,490/yr = 2 months free)
Everything in Plus — and then the door closes behind you.

You become the **only** business featured in your category. Always **#1**. The **sole Official Partner** of that category. This isn't a bigger badge; it's exclusivity, and it's **strictly one per category** — physically enforced in our system so it **cannot be oversold.** When it's yours, there is no "next one up." A competitor can't buy their way past you because the slot doesn't exist for them.

There are only as many of these as there are categories — 10 in total. When your category's seat is taken, it's taken.
