# Lompoc Locals — Sales & Marketing Kit

Everything you need to sell Lompoc Locals memberships — online, on the street,
and everywhere. Each document is standalone; together they're the full story:
who we are, the opportunity, what a business gets, how to pitch it, and the
printable pieces to leave behind.

> **The one-line pitch:** *Lompoc Locals is the town's local hub — directory, map,
> events, and deals. Become an Official Partner and the whole hub works for your
> business: top of your category, your deal featured across the site, and the badge
> locals trust. One spot per category.*

## How to use this kit
1. **Warm up** with the market research + brand story (know why this matters).
2. **On the street / a call:** run the [sales playbook](04-sales-playbook.md), show the
   live site on your phone, hand over the [one-pager](one-pager.html).
3. **Online / inbound:** point to the [media kit](06-media-kit.md) and tiers.
4. **Working with a marketing vendor:** give them the [methodology](05-methodology-for-vendors.md).
5. **Presenting:** open the [pitch deck](pitch-deck.html) full-screen.
