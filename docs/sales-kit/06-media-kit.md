## What Lompoc Locals is

Lompoc Locals is the single, local-only hub for one town: Lompoc + Vandenberg, ZIPs 93436, 93437, and 93438. Not a county aggregator, not a national directory with a Lompoc tab — a home built for this community alone.

In one place, it brings together **a living business directory, an interactive map, a daily-synced events calendar, a growing local blog, and a weekly digest** — in both English and Spanish. It's where a neighbor finds a new lunch spot, a family plans the weekend, a visitor figures out what's in town, and a business gets found and gets remembered.

## Who it reaches, and how

Reach here is about **discovery** — being the place people land when they're actively looking for something local. That happens four ways:

**Locals searching for what's in town.** Residents come to the hub to answer "who does X in Lompoc?" — food, wine, services, shopping. Depth is what makes those searches land, and the directory runs deep: **476 approved businesses across 10 categories.**

**Search and SEO for Lompoc queries.** The hub is built to rank for the local searches people actually type. **48 SEO blog posts** and **144 curated profiles** feed that engine, pulling in people who searched Google for something in Lompoc and found the hub.

**The interactive map.** Instead of a list of addresses, visitors explore the town visually — pins, categories, and branded markers that turn "somewhere nearby" into "right there."

**The events hub — including rocket launches.** A daily-synced calendar of **96 upcoming events**, from local happenings to **Vandenberg rocket launches** — a genuinely unique draw that pulls in both locals and visitors watching for the next launch window.

Together that's the **local word-of-mouth position, made digital** — the place people already trust to tell them what's happening and who to go to.
