# Tier-1 First-Deal Suggestions

Walk-in ready. Each block maps to the deal form fields (`type`, `title`, `discount_text`,
`description`, `terms`). Pitch it as: *"Here's a first deal I drafted for you — want to run
this, or tweak it?"* Keep the first deal **low-cost to them and easy to say yes to** — the
goal is a live offer, not a margin hit. Suggested run: ~4 weeks, then they swap it.

Each business has a **primary** (recommended) and an **alt** in case they balk at the first.

## How to use these in the pitch

1. Pull up their page on your phone (`/en/biz/<slug>`), claim it.
2. Open "Add deal," pick the **type**, paste **title / discount text / description / terms**.
3. Set expiry ~4 weeks out. Hit publish. Show them the live page.
4. That's the "you're live 🎉" handoff moment — ask them to reshare your spotlight post.

> These are starting points sized to be painless for the owner. If they want richer margins,
> the **alt** is usually lower-cost to them. Let *them* land on the final number — ownership
> makes them more likely to promote it.
