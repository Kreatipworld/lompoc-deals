# Content Plan — Month 1 (batch & schedule)

8 posts across 4 weeks (2/week), built on the 4 pillars from the acquisition plan:
**Deal of the week · Business spotlight · Lompoc local · Milestone.**

Batch-write these once, schedule them (Meta Business Suite lets you queue FB + IG together),
then your only weekly job is replying to comments and dropping spotlights into local FB
groups. Spotlights are timed to follow onboarding — **publish a spotlight the same week a
business goes live** so the tag-and-reshare loop fires while they're excited.

**Channels:** Facebook page + Instagram (queue together) + paste into local FB groups
("Lompoc Community Board," "Lompoc Buy/Sell/Trade," "What's Happening in Lompoc") where group
rules allow promo. Facebook is the priority — that's where Lompoc's community actually is.

**Hashtag block (reuse, trim to ~8 on IG):**
`#LompocDeals #Lompoc #LompocCA #OldTownLompoc #ShopLocalLompoc #SupportLocal #CentralCoast #805 #VandenbergSFB`

> Anything in **[brackets]** is a swap-in — replace with the actual business that went live
> that week. The spotlight order below assumes you land the three social-ready Tier-1 spots
> (South Side Coffee, Old Town Kitchen, Lompoc Barber Lounge) first, since they'll reshare.

---

## Week 1 — Launch

### Post 1.1 — Announcement (the "we exist" post)
- **Pillar:** Announcement / Milestone
- **Image:** Simple branded graphic — "Lompoc Deals" logo + tagline over an Old Town / flower
  fields photo.
- **Caption:**
  > Lompoc, meet your new deals feed. 🌼
  > We're rounding up the best coupons, specials, and happy hours from *local* Lompoc spots —
  > all in one place, free to browse.
  > 👉 Check it out: lompoc-deals.vercel.app
  > Local business owner? Get your spot listed free — DM us. We're setting up the first 25
  > businesses ourselves. 🤝
- **Notes:** Pin this to the top of your FB page. Post to all three groups.

### Post 1.2 — Lompoc local (engagement bait)
- **Pillar:** Lompoc local
- **Image:** Fun question graphic or a photo of a local breakfast burrito.
- **Caption:**
  > Settle a debate for us, Lompoc 👇
  > **Best breakfast burrito in town — who's got it?**
  > Drop the name. We're hungry and we're taking notes. 🌯
- **Notes:** Pure reach play — no link, just comments. Reply to every answer; it trains the
  algorithm and starts relationships with the businesses people name.

## After Month 1

- Keep the rhythm: ~1 spotlight + 1 deal/community post per week, always fed by who you
  onboarded that week. No inventing content.
- Once ~15 businesses are live and the feed looks active, that's the **Phase 2 trigger** —
  flip toward locals: push the weekly email digest and lean harder into the FB groups.
- Save your best-performing post format and repeat it. Let the data pick your style.
