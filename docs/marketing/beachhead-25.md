# Beachhead 25 — Founding Business Target List

**Generated:** 2026-06-23 from the live business DB (unclaimed seed listings).
**Goal:** get 12–15 of these *live with a real deal* in ~8 weeks. Work Tier 1 first.

How to read it: every business below is already in the directory as an *unclaimed* listing
(owned by the scraper account), so onboarding = claiming + posting their first deal. The
`slug` is their page: `https://lompoc-deals.vercel.app/en/biz/<slug>`.

**Chains and medical/real-estate listings were deliberately excluded** — no local
decision-maker, or not deal-friendly. These 25 are all independent, foot-traffic, and
discount-friendly.

## Notes & data flags

- **Pitch scripts** are in the design spec:
  `docs/superpowers/specs/2026-06-23-business-acquisition-plan-design.md`.
- A few directory listings carried fake `555-01xx` phone numbers (likely placeholder seed
  data) — **excluded** from this list. Verify any phone before relying on it.
- Some names had near-duplicate entries in the DB (e.g. Capulín Eats appears twice). When you
  claim one, flag the dupe to delete so the public directory stays clean.
- When a business goes live, update the Status box to ☑ and note in the tracking sheet
  whether they reshared the spotlight.
