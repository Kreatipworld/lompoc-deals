# Lompoc Activation Day — Local Channel Playbook

**Created:** 2026-06-26
**Goal:** Fill an activation day with locals and businesses — maximum legitimate reach across Lompoc + Vandenberg (ZIPs 93436 / 93437 / 93438), no harvested email lists.

## The core idea: businesses are the megaphone

The fastest path to a packed event is **not** reaching 10,000 strangers cold — it's getting the ~330 Lompoc businesses already in our directory to each promote the day to *their own* customers and storefront foot traffic. 330 businesses × their audiences dwarfs any scraped blast, and it's all consent-based (each business chooses to share with its own list). Everything below feeds that flywheel.

> **Compliance guardrail:** Every email we send goes only to (a) businesses' own public contact addresses for B2B participation outreach, or (b) people who opted in via our site. Every send carries a real physical address + one-click unsubscribe (CAN-SPAM). No harvested consumer lists, ever.

---

## Channels (Lompoc + Vandenberg only)

### Tier 1 — Institutional partners (start here, longest lead time)
- **Lompoc Valley Chamber of Commerce** — co-promote to membership; ask for newsletter mention + event-calendar listing; potential co-host.
- **City of Lompoc** — Parks, Recreation & Urban Forestry events calendar; request permits early; ask for inclusion in city comms.
- **Lompoc Record** — local newspaper: pitch a community-event story (earned media) + a calendar listing; consider one print/online ad close to the date.
- **Lompoc Valley Festival Association / Old Town Lompoc** — tie into existing Old Town foot traffic (Mural Walk, Friday farmers market).
- **Lompoc Unified School District** — flyers in backpacks / parent newsletters (great for family turnout; follow district approval process).
- **Vandenberg SFB** — Airman & Family Readiness / base community bulletin (huge local population; respect base posting rules).

### Tier 2 — Owned + social (we control these)
- **Existing weekly digest** — feature the event for several issues leading up; add a prominent banner on the homepage and a dedicated event page with RSVP/opt-in.
- **Local Facebook groups** — "Lompoc Community," "What's Happening in Lompoc," buy/sell/trade groups. Post as a community event (respect group rules; partner with admins).
- **Instagram / TikTok** — short clips with participating businesses; reshare businesses' posts; a shared event hashtag.
- **KLOM / local radio** — community-calendar mentions; possible live remote on the day.

### Tier 3 — Physical / on-the-ground
- **Flyers + window posters** in the 330 participating businesses (give each a printable kit).
- **Lompoc Public Library**, community center (Dick DeWees), parks bulletin boards.
- **Old Town Friday farmers market** — table/booth in the weeks prior.
- **Yard signs / banner** at high-traffic intersections (check city sign rules).

---

## Timeline (count back from event date = T)

- **T-8 to T-6 weeks:** Lock date/venue + permits. Recruit Tier-1 partners. Send the **business participation email** (B2B outreach to the collected list) inviting businesses to join/sponsor. Stand up the event landing page + RSVP opt-in.
- **T-5 to T-3 weeks:** Confirm participating businesses; ship each a promo kit (flyer PDF, social graphics, suggested captions, window poster). Pitch Lompoc Record + radio. Begin digest features. Submit to all event calendars.
- **T-2 weeks:** Social push (us + every participating business reshares). Flyers go up in all storefronts. School/base channels. Farmers-market table.
- **T-1 week:** Press reminder, radio mentions, final digest blast to opted-in list, countdown posts.
- **Day-of:** Live social, encourage businesses to post stories, hashtag, collect emails on-site **with consent** (signup sheet / QR to the RSVP page) for next time.

---

## Messaging
- **To businesses (B2B):** "Be part of Lompoc's activation day — free to join, we drive foot traffic to your door. Here's your promo kit." (Self-interest = they'll promote it.)
- **To locals (via opt-in + partners):** community, family-friendly, local pride, deals/specials from businesses they know.

## What "success" looks like (track these)
- # businesses recruited (target as % of the 330).
- # RSVPs / opt-ins captured on the landing page.
- Tier-1 partner placements secured (Chamber, city calendar, Record, base).
- Day-of attendance + new consented signups collected on-site.

## Explicitly NOT doing
- No scraping/harvesting of personal/consumer emails.
- No emailing anyone who didn't opt in or isn't a business we're inviting to participate.
- Nothing outside Lompoc + Vandenberg (93436/37/38).
